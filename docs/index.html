Knabberfuchs

An honest calorie tracker.

Scan a barcode, search, snap a photo of your meal, or just type a name and the calories. No ads, no subscription, no popups, no account. Works offline, and your data never leaves your phone.

Ad-free No subscription No popups No account Works offline Your data stays local

What it does

📷

Scan barcodes

Packaged products from Open Food Facts. Not in the database? Add it yourself — scan the nutrition label and it's saved for next time.

🔎

Fast local search

Hundreds of thousands of foods from Open Food Facts, plus the curated Swiss Food Composition Database — instant, with smart ranking.

📸

Recognise a meal

Snap a photo and on-device AI guesses the dish and estimates the calories. No cloud, no account — it runs entirely on your phone.

⚡

Quick add

Already know the number? Log “Lasagna · 816 kcal” in a couple of taps, with optional macros.

📖

Recipes

Build recipes once, then log a portion to any day — broken back into its real ingredients. Share by QR or image, import by QR or text.

🖼️

Meal from a photo

Photograph a printed ingredient list; on-device OCR turns it into a meal you can match and log.

🍽️

Effortless meals

Everything you add flows into the current meal automatically. Rename it, split it across days, or save it as a recipe.

🌍

Offline, worldwide

Download any of 106 country databases and scan + search with no connection.

🗣️

Speaks your language

Available in English, German, French and Italian.

❤️

Health Connect

Optionally sync your logged calories & macros to Android Health Connect.

💾

Your data, a zip

Export everything to a backup zip (JSON + CSV) and restore it any time. No cloud, no lock-in.

Built on Open Food Facts ❤️

Knabberfuchs is built on Open Food Facts — a free, open, crowdsourced food database kept alive by volunteers around the world. Without their work, this app wouldn't exist.
