Food & nutrition data

Swiss Food Composition Database

Whole-food nutrient values come from the Swiss Food Composition Database, published by the Swiss Federal Food Safety and Veterinary Office (FSVO / BLV). Used with attribution in line with its terms of use. Source: naehrwertdaten.ch.

Food & nutrition data

USDA FoodData Central

Typical serving and portion sizes are derived from USDA FoodData Central, a U.S. Department of Agriculture resource released into the public domain.

On-device AI

Google AIY “food_V1” model

On-device meal-photo recognition uses Google's AIY food_V1 image-classification model, bundled and run entirely on your phone. Licensed under the Apache License 2.0.
